Create a high-fidelity, interactive UI prototype for a web application called “ET IntelliBrief – AI Newsroom”.

The prototype should simulate a working product, not just static screens.

---

OVERALL CONCEPT:
A next-generation business news experience that transforms articles into interactive AI-generated intelligence briefings.

The design must combine:

* Editorial newspaper elegance (typography)
* News channel dynamics (breaking alerts, ticker)
* Modern AI dashboard clarity

The UI should feel premium, futuristic, and highly interactive.

---

THEME & STYLE:

* Dark theme with subtle gradients OR soft off-white editorial theme
* Accent colors:

  * Red (breaking news)
  * Blue (AI/finance)
* Clean grid layout
* High contrast
* Minimal but powerful

---

TYPOGRAPHY:

* Headlines → Serif (editorial/news feel)
* Body → Sans-serif (clean UI)
* Strong hierarchy

---

SCREENS TO CREATE:

---

1. LANDING SCREEN (INPUT PAGE)

Components:

* Logo: ET IntelliBrief

* Tagline: “Your AI-Powered Newsroom”

* Large centered input field:
  Placeholder: “Enter topic (e.g., Budget 2026, Startup Funding)”

* Persona selection (interactive buttons):

  * Investor
  * Founder
  * Student

* CTA button:
  “Generate Briefing”

INTERACTION:

* Clicking “Generate” → go to loading screen

---

2. LOADING SCREEN

Components:

* Animated loader
* Text:
  “Analyzing news… Generating intelligence briefing…”

INTERACTION:

* After 2–3 seconds → auto transition to output screen

---

3. AI NEWSROOM OUTPUT SCREEN

TOP BAR:

* Red breaking strip:
  “Breaking: AI-generated briefing ready”

* Optional scrolling ticker

---

MAIN CONTENT:

HEADLINE SECTION:

* Large serif headline
* Short TL;DR summary

---

INSIGHT GRID (card layout):

Cards for:

* Key Points
* Winners
* Losers
* Market Impact
* Why It Matters
* What Next
* For You (highlighted card)

Cards should have:

* Rounded corners
* Soft shadows
* Hover effects

---

INTERACTION:

* Cards slightly expand on hover

---

4. CHAT SECTION

* Input field: “Ask anything about this news”
* Chat bubbles (user + AI response)

INTERACTION:

* Simulate 1–2 pre-filled chat responses

---

5. VIDEO SECTION

* Button:
  “Generate 60-sec Video Briefing”

INTERACTION:

* On click → show loading spinner
* Then reveal video preview card

VIDEO UI:

* Video player placeholder
* Reporter/avatar at bottom corner
* Headline overlay style

---

6. BREAKING MODE EFFECT (IMPORTANT)

When output loads:

* Red top bar appears
* Subtle animation (slide or fade)
* Optional glow effect

---

PROTOTYPE FLOW:

Landing → Click Generate → Loading → Output Screen →
→ Click Video → Video appears

---

VISUAL DETAILS:

* Smooth transitions (fade, slide)
* Clean spacing and alignment
* Use icons for each section
* Data visualization placeholders (charts, graphs)

---

GOAL:

When presenting, the prototype should feel like a real working product where:

* User inputs a topic
* AI generates insights
* User explores and interacts
* Video briefing appears dynamically

---

FINAL FEEL:

“Bloomberg + Netflix + AI assistant”

NOT:

* blog
* static UI
* traditional news website

---

Make it visually stunning, intuitive, and futuristic.
